# QRV.Network Production Content Strategy

## Platform Position
QR-V™ is a registry-based verification infrastructure that turns QR codes into verifiable references. A QR-V scan should resolve through a controlled verification process, not an arbitrary destination.

## Core Message
Verify records. Confirm authenticity. Trust the issuer.
